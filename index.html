Why Kenya

Kenya is the origin of the safari concept. The whole Idea started here during the colonial days. Our country is so diverse, from the dry deserts In the North, the mountains and the forests in the central Kenya, the great rift valley that splits Africa in to two halves the open savannah grasslands in the south to the beautiful beaches on our coast line that stretches for over three hundred and fifty Kilometers long.

TOM NJOGU SAFARIS

Having been born in Nanyuki, right on the slopes of Mt Kenya and brought up in a farmland adjacent to Mt Kenya forest. I grew up watching and interacting with wildlife on a daily basis through out my young age. On my way to school and back at home, from elephants, Giraffes, ostriches and all plain game found in the Savanah grasslands. My curiosity on animals planted a seed in me that would come to be my Job, career, hobby and simply my lifestyle. I have been guiding people on safari for over seventeen years. Passionately the curiosity never ends as every day on safari is different.Kenya is very rich in Flora and Fauna
